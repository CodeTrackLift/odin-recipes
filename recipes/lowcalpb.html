Low Calorie Peanut Butter

Ingredients

  • PB2 Powdered Peanut Butter, 18g
  • Cookie Butter, Frosted Cinnamon Roll Flavor, 6g
  • Cinnamon 0.25g
  • Salt 0.1g
  • Water

Directions:

  1. Add dry ingredients
  2. Slowly add water and stir until desired texture is met
