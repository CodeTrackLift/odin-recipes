French Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Bread, 3 Slices
  • Egg Whites, 270g
  • Cinnamon, 1g
  • Salt, 0.5g
  • Splenda, 3 packets
  • Vanilla Extract, 1 tsp
  • Pam Cooking Spray
  • Walden Farms Pancake Syrup
  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add wet ingredients, mix until even color
  3. Soak bread for 20 mins
  4. Set stove top to medium-low heat
  5. Apply cooking spray to pan
  6. Gently place soaked bread on pan
  7. Cook each side until lightly browned, or cooked to preference
  8. Add syrup and enjoy!
